The Madras Day Celebrations


A city deserves to celebrate its birthday. And this year, Madras celebrates its 375th birthday. Madras Day was an idea that three people put together - the city’s famed historian S. Muthiah, journalist Sashi Nair and publisher Vincent D’ Souza. Later, they were joined by three others - senior journalist & editor Sushila Ravindranath, journalist & web site entrepreneur Revathi R and entrepreneur & writer-historian V. Sriram! And this year, QoP joins hands with them. We had asked the writers to share their anecdotes, tales, quirks, reminiscences, anything that they would like to share about the city of Madras - in exactly 375 words - no more no less. So, here are the winners!
