About Us


Quill on Parchment, or QoP as we would like to call it, had its initial seeds sown when a group of 20-somethings sat down to crib about how they did not have a customized platform to exhibit their literary interests. Few people adore literature in its glorious essence and fewer people make an attempt to create their own literature. In order to ensure a niche space for amateurs who strive to think beyond the staple desi-novels, we decided to bring to you QoP. QoP is for the novice who is inspired to write for the love of itself. It is to show the way forward to those who love art in the form of poetry and prose. As an added impetus to this exciting venture, the team at QoP is in talks with a few publishing houses who might be willing to bring out the Best of Literature at QoP, in the form of a book. Currently, QoP boasts of sections to let your words adorn our page in the form of Poems, Stories, Travelogues, Food-blogs and Reviews. You may record your feedback and suggestions here and help us broaden our repertoire!
