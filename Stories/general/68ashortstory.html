Working hard does not always pay off, regardless of what they tell you. Some people can work so hard, that their soul bleeds out, and still not rake in any rewards. Others get it as easy as a simple snap of fingers. The world’s not fair.

And it’s not the talented people I envy. It’s the hardworking, motivated ones. Because even knowing all of this, they’re still there, giving it their all, and that’s simply incredible.

But sometimes you work so hard, ignoring everything else, and when you achieved everything you wanted, and you go home at night and you just sort of lay there, staring at your ceiling, you kind of wonder: what do I actually have? All that I’ve worked so hard to achieved, where’s its orth?
