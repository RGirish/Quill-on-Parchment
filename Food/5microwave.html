1. Making ghee- Keep 1 ½ - 2 cups malai in a big glass bowl and microwave for 10-15 minutes to get desi ghee without burning your kadai. Stir once or twice in between.

2. Warming baby’s milk bottle- Do check the temperature of the milk on your inner wrist. The bottle will not become hot, while the milk will.

3. To roast one tbsp. of cashews, spread on a micro proof plate and microwave for one minute to get golden roasted cashews.

4. To roast papad, place 4 papads on a paper napkin and microwave for 1 ½ minutes. Turn side once in between.

5. Dissolving gelatin- Soak 2 tbsp gelatin with 4 tbsp water for 10 minutes. Microwave for 15-20 seconds to dissolve.

6. Boiling potatoes- Wash potatoes to wet them. Place them in microwave steamer on the turn table. Microwave for 5 minutes for 4 medium potatoes. Similarly microwave corn for 3 minutes and half Kg Arbi for 6 minutes.

7. Making khattti-mithi chutney- Mix 1 tbsp mango powder, 3 tbsp sugar 1/3 cup water along with spices in a glass bowl. Microwave for 3 minutes, stirring in between.

8. Making dry bread crumbs from fresh bread- Microwave 4 slices of bread for 2 minutes. Change side and microwave for another minute. Give some standing for the bread to dry out and then grind in a mixer to get crumbs.

9. Freshening stale chips, biscuits, or corn flakes- Place the chips / biscuits in a napkin, uncovered for about 1 minute per bowl or until they feel warm. Wait for a few minutes to allow cooling and serve.

10. Blanching almonds to remove skin- Put almonds in a small bowl of water and microwave for 2 minutes or till water just starts to boil. After the water cools, the almonds can be peeled very easily.
